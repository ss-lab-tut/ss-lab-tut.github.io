Our laboratory brings together networking and artificial intelligence to realize the next generation of information and communication systems.

Research Topics

We combine theory, simulation, implementation, and data analysis across the following areas:

  • Edge AI
  • Federated Learning
  • IoT
  • Semantic Communications
  • Disaster-Resilient Networks
  • Elderly Care Monitoring Systems
  • Service Function Chaining (SFC)

Who We Are Looking For

Programming experience and domain knowledge matter, but what we value most is the willingness to learn on your own, try things, think critically, and move research forward.

We welcome students interested in networking, AI, IoT, and information systems that address real-world social challenges.
